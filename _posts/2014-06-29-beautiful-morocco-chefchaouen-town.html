Chefchaouen, an old city in northern Morocco. Almost everything here is painted in shades of blue. The city was built in the 1400s and welcomed Jewish people over the centuries. The blue color comes from Jewish tradition that teaches that prayer shawls dyed with tekhelel (a natural blue dye) are a great reminder of God's power.
