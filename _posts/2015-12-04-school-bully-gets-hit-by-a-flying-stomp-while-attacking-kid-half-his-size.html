Thank you world for the proliferation of video-capturing gadgets, so you bullies out there can be recognised for what you really are. When the entire world watched and cheered as Casey Heynes body slammed his aggressor against the concrete, he became a poster boy for anti-bullying worldwide and many victims have since followed in his footsteps.

Not every child crawls up into a defenceless ball, crying and pleading for their antagonist to leave them alone. No, no, no, on the contrary, a few brave souls stand up and defend themselves.

Enter this kid.

 

He was initially picked on for “talking so much shit all the time,” although he begs to differ. His attacker doesn’t even give him a chance to take off his rucksack before repeatedly punching him and dragging him to the ground. But the boy in red wasn’t about to get his arse whooped without putting up a fight.

 

Although he tries to protect himself as much as he can, the bully gets on top (literally) and starts to give him a proper pummelling. That is until a red dressed vigilante decides to take a stand.

Despite the fact that there were numerous onlookers checking out the fight, no one bothered to step in. Until the boy’s saviour entered the scene and hit the bully with a double footed flying stomp in the back.

That’s got to hurt.
