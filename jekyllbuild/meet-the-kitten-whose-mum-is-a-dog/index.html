Meet The Kitten Whose Mum Is A Dog

When Rosie the kitten was picked up on the streets of LA, the three sisters who found her feared the worst. The three-week-old puss was on her last legs and rejected the girls’ attempts to feed her. In a last ditch effort to save the cat’s life, the sisters turned to one of their pet huskies, Lilo, for help.

There was every chance that the husky would kill the kitten, given their natural predatory instincts, but, happily, Lilo had other ideas. She’d been unable to have pups of her own and took Rosie in as if she was her own child, allowing her to suckle and protecting her.

Here they are together and it’s unbelievably adorable…

Lilo now has an Instagram account with 41,000 followers so you can keep up with Rosie to your heart’s content.
